The importance of coral reefs

The coral reefs help us in more ways than we can imagine:

They protect costal homes from storms and floods

Coral reefs reduce the impact of huge waves and storms on costal cities. They help prevent erosion, flooding and life loss! Without coral reefs, storms will have a much stronger impact on costal cities.

They bring in millions of dollars from tourists

282 million dollars are produced from ocean based cities are activities

The oceans and coral reefs produce 50% of the oxygen that we breath

The oceans provide us with many medical products

Ingredients that can help fight cancer, athritis, Alzheimer's disease, and heart disease are all found in the coral reefs.

Provides us with food

The ocean doesn't only prodived us with seafood. Many foods such as peanut butter and soy milk contain ingredients from the sea.
